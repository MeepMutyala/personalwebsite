What's Been Going On?
What have I been up to?

To record a little update on what’s been going on:

I joined this amazing program called the Delaware Youth Leadership Network(DYLN) that started late in the summer. In DYLN, high school juniors solve design challenges and practice creative problem solving while learning leadership skills. This year we had one big design challenge of coordinating a Diamond Challenge pitch event. The Diamond Challenge is an entrepreneurship competition where high school students create and pitch their business ideas to a panel of judges. Think Shark Tank. We just finished hosting the competition and the months of hard work paid off; it was a huge success.

Speaking of entrepreneurship, I started an Entrepreneurship Club at my school this year. Basically we brainstorm business ideas, research entrepreneurship skills, practice presenting and create our own small businesses. One of our groups ended up competing at the Diamond Challenge this year.

I continue to serve as the Delaware TSA State Vice President, an opportunity I will never get tired of. As chair of the Leadership Committee this year, I created and launched a program called the State Committee Mentoring Program(SCMP). SCMP aims to build leadership within the students in TSA by bringing them onto our state committees and mentoring them. Furthermore, it increases our state’s workforce on our committees, allowing us to get a lot more done. This year we have 5 mentees, and they’ve been cooking up some awesome things. We also have a lot of cool stuff ready for our state conference next month.

Thanks for reading!
