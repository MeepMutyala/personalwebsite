Reflections


Reflections.
Hello!

It’s been almost a year since my last post. I stopped posting because, a little over a year ago, I came across an obstacle that I thought was going to change the course of everything in my life. I didn’t have any control over it, and I felt like my world was crashing down around me. So I didn’t feel like I was in the best place to continue this blog.

But life kept moving on. People went on with their lives, school wasn’t letting up on the work, and I was expected to continue my routine. Only I didn’t feel like it. I resisted and dwelled on the past for much too long. I thought about what went wrong and how I could have fixed it, or how far back in the past I would go to make things right. How different my life would be if I solved the issue.

Eventually, I came to realize how I wasn’t changing anything by continuing to brood over what happened. It was done, and it was set in stone. Reflecting on it is important, but not accepting what happened is not. I finally resolved to keep moving forward.

And this situation turned out not to have affected my life as negatively as I thought. Instead, it opened the door to new opportunities for me. I was able to learn and achieve much more as a result.

It’s important to reflect, but not brood.

Blogs will continue to be posted bimonthly(every other month) and they will be much shorter and concise like this one hopefully was! This project is overall a reflection of my past so I can learn and understand greater than before. It’s mainly for me, but if you’re reading, thanks for being a part of it.

-Apu Mutyala
