Photography and Videography

"Taking pictures is savoring life intensely, every hundredth of a second."

- Marc Riboud

My passion for videography started when I was in middle school, and I produced videos for various creative projects and competitions. After begging my parents to buy me a video editing software, I was enthralled with the number of ways I can create and edit videos to actively engage people and was amazed.

I started experimenting with photography after a while of making videos. After all, I figured, I have this quality DSLR camera, I should make the most of it! I grew more and more interested and I decided to dive in. Although I may be considered an amateur, my work has become high quality and I guarantee that you will be satisfied with my services.

Technology Student Association (TSA)

“TSA is an organization based on inspiring students to further the future of STEM and technology.”

The Technology Student Association is a STEM-oriented student organization that enhances leadership and technical skills within students. In an ever growing technological based society, it is important that we lead interested students into various STEM fields so that they may invent, discover, and innovate to advance the future.

Leadership Experiences

"If your actions inspire others to dream more, learn more, do more and become more, you are a leader."

- John Quincy Adams

In any organization, group, or team, each member must have a sense of leadership. Leadership doesn’t just mean taking charge and commanding. Leadership is being responsible, having communication skills, and being able to collaborate with others. As a state officer of TSA, class senator of my school, and other experiences, I've learned a lot about leadership. And I'm glad that it has given me access to an invaluable set of skills.
